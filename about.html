About

Bio

Nearly Two Decades
of Craft

Sabrina Texidor is a Los Angeles-based actor, writer, and director with nearly two decades of experience and the kind of warmth and energy that tends to stay with you long after the credits roll.

"In Sabrina we saw an energy and exuberance that shines through and is absolutely infectious."

— Ayo Davis, SVP Talent & Casting, ABC Entertainment

A 2017 ABC Discovers U.S. Digital Talent Competition Winner, she is a SAG-AFTRA member whose television work includes a recurring guest star role on ABC's Grand Hotel, a guest star appearance on CBS's S.W.A.T., and a co-star role on CBS's Scorpion. On stage, she has led productions of West Side Story, Once Upon a Mattress, and Fiddler on the Roof — roles that reflect the same range and joy she brings to every performance.

Bilingual in English and Spanish, Sabrina trained at Lesly Kahn and Company, the Ivana Chubbuck Studio, and with master teachers Deborah Aquila and Bob Krakower. She holds a degree in Communications and Media Studies from Fordham University — with a minor in Philosophy, because she has always been as curious about why we tell stories as she is about how.

Off screen, you might find her on a dance floor (salsa is non-negotiable), lost in a good book, or somewhere unexpected — Morocco, South Africa, Colombia, Italy, Venezuela, Paraguay, England. She travels not just to see the world, but to understand it, and that curiosity finds its way into every character she plays.

Now expanding into writing and directing, Sabrina is building work that is as layered and alive as she is.
