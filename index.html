About

Storyteller.
Shapeshifter.

Sabrina Texidor is a Los Angeles-based actor, writer, and director with nearly two decades of experience and the kind of warmth and energy that tends to stay with you long after the credits roll.

A 2017 ABC Discovers U.S. Digital Talent Competition Winner, she is a SAG-AFTRA member with recurring and guest star credits on ABC and CBS, and a creative voice driven by stories that live at the intersection of identity, power, and the human spirit.
